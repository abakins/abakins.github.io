- I'm currently a member of research staff in the Microwave Instrument Science Group at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory and a visiting researcher at Caltech

- I have a B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Georgia Tech. My Ph.D. advisor was Paul Steffes
